Ashley Hefnawy

Ashley Hefnawy is a 27-year-old Egyptian American writer working and living in New York. She works as a freelance copywriter,specializing in product, marketing, and web copy with all the tech companies.

Her experience being a person of color in the industry, She found a lot other POC to learn from. She learned about copywriting, design, creativity, and innovation,

came to her from women, particularly women of color, who had been in the industry longer than her, who were down to share their knowledge.
